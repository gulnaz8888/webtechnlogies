Club Managers

Ulan Maksat – organizes the Fantasy Club. He is passionate about Tolkien and the world of magical adventures. Ulan loves guiding readers through epic quests, mythical creatures, and heroic journeys, making every meeting feel like a trip to another realm.

The Fantasy Club invites readers to explore magical worlds, epic adventures, and legendary heroes. From Tolkien’s Middle-earth to modern fantasy novels, members share their love for imagination, mythical creatures, and storytelling that transports us beyond reality.

Aknur Ondasyn – hosts the Classics Club. She is always ready to discuss world literature, from ancient epics to modern masterpieces. Aknur values deep conversations about themes, characters, and history, inspiring members to see timeless works in a new light.

The Classics Club is dedicated to timeless masterpieces of world literature. Here, members revisit great authors, analyze historical contexts, and discuss how classical works continue to shape our understanding of culture, society, and human nature.

Gulnaz Yeskermes – hosts the Romance Club.She enjoys exploring stories about love, emotions, and human relationships. Gulnaz creates a warm, welcoming atmosphere where members can share feelings, discuss heartfelt novels, and connect over timeless tales of passion and devotion.

The Romance Club is a space for those who love stories of love, passion, and emotional journeys. Members explore both classic and contemporary romance novels, discussing themes of relationships, personal growth, and the universal search for connection.

How to Join

  1. Fill out the registration form.
  2. Choose your favorite genre club and write it to the message line.
  3. Managers will contact you in a few days with explanations.
  4. Attend the first meeting.
